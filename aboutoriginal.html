Recording engineer and producer Joe Hernandez founded innovative Music Studios in 2004. Innovative Music Studios started outas a single room in Soho, Manhattan studio that promoted itself mainly by word of mouth. As word started to spread, Innovative Music began to build its clientele and is now in a 4-room studio recording facility in heart of Midtown, Manhattan.

Innovative Music's mission is to help musicians reach their goals by helping with recording, mixing and mastering as well as help educating musician with the many subjects in music production.Innovative music, is a music production company that offers a variety of recording services such as recording, mixing, mastering, songwriting and music
